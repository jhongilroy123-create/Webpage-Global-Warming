Ozone Depletion & Ecosystem Risks

The stratospheric ozone depletion over Antarctica increases harmful UV-B radiation exposure, especially as the ozone hole now persists into early summer when plants and animals are most vulnerable. Climate change worsens this by causing earlier ice and snowmelt, heatwaves, and droughts, amplifying UV-B impacts on fragile ecosystems.
